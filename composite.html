Area 51

The concept I did for my Composite Art Piece was the mysterious and unknown world of Area 51. I felt that by going on Google Maps I would get the sense of presence. Yet, comparing the various areas I google mapped for the excerises I was able to actually move the little yellow dude to be able to walk. Yet, when roaming around my little yellow dude was on an alien space ship roaming around. My art piece is my interpretation of what happens in Area 51 which it has been known for being an alien impacted area where they supposedly negotiate with the US Army. Also has been known to conduct all extraterrestrial activities there. The techniques used for my assignment were the magnetic lasso as well for the rectangular marquee tool. I also played with the saturation of colors with the beam of the alien space ship and the map view as well. The thing I found interesting was that the lake on satellite view looks like a lake yet when going map view it shows like an X. Very odd. Overall I hope my piece plays with the unknown of area 51.
