Coachella Glitched

The concept I explored in this project was Glitch Art and Net Art. I choose these mediums because my favorite was glitch art because it turns out colorful and nice. I also decided to put it on the web so that we can see the image transformed into a web background instead of a board with four images. I also gave a little background of the image. One difficulty was that because I glitched the original so much even when having the original file untouched I could not upload. So I had to get images of art installation from the web even though the glitch art came from my own images of the art installations.
