Four Seasong

My concept with this video is to show people who will see my installation what Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter look like to me. Living in California all my life I haven't really experienced dramatic season changes but I have been to other states during the seasons change where the are looks like this. My installation would be in a room that would have one projector. The music mixed with my video really livens up the room which is what I want the feel to be with anyone who is watching my video.
